Welcome To The Ambassadors Schools, Ota

Where God Helps Us To Build A Total Child

The Ambassadors Schools, established in 1998, is a Christian co-educational school that is driven by a God-ordained unction to manifest greatly in the younger generation. We are here to correct the misconception that proclaims this generation of youth as a wasted one and rebrand the Nigerian youth to become worthy ambassadors and reputable leaders.

The right of a youth to learn is our responsibility to uphold. The uniqueness of their enthusiastic minds brings forth a craving to learn, and it is this craving that we satisfy. We harness the depth of talent embedded in the youth and mould the youth for the performance of exploits.

At The Ambassadors College, we do not just teach our wards but train them to become the proverbial "Renaissance man," and we also groom them to become pathfinders, inventors, and problem solvers.
We are what your child needs to attain a God-ordained destiny.

Know More

Our Vision

To foster the academic, spiritual, physical, and emotional development of every Child.

Our Mission

As a Christian school, we provide solid functional literacy for students through the blending of biblical and moral principles, plus a value-added guidance and counselling with emphasis on qualitative co-curricular activities.

Our Path

We lead our students into the future by connecting them with higher institutions locally and internationally with the help of capable and well-informed Admission Officers. By doing this, we relieve our parents of the stress of first-degree admission processes within and outside the country.
